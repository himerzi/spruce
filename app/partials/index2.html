Thoughts create emotions

It's not what happens to you, but how you think about it that makes you feel the way you do. By changing the automatic thoughts you have in response to circumstances, you can change the way you feel.

Change your thoughts

Use Spruce with a troubling concern to challenge your irrational thoughts and let go of anxiety and sadness. How? We'll ask you a series of questions about an upsetting situation, then we'll give you an exercise to help you revise your thoughts.

Keep a journal

You can go back to what you write at any time to review and consolidate your new more rational thinking. This helps in similar situations in the future.


Convenient

You can start using the tool immediately. You can return to it at any time you need to. We've found that people use it about 2-3 times per week and feel the benefit after their first use.

Confidential

We understand that there are some things you want to talk about, and some you want to keep to yourself. Spruce is totally privately, so you can use it without worrying if your issues are embarassing or just trivial.

Who for?

Anyone who is feeling stressed, troubled or upset. It's effective for minor niggling issues like stress at work as well as more serious problems such as depression.

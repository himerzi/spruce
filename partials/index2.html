What does it do?

Spruce helps you combat your negative thoughts and emotions. Spruce can help you to deal with your feelings of unwarranted sadness and anxiety in safe and convenient manner.

How does Spruce work?

We take you through a guided process that helps you identify the thoughts at the root of your troubling emotion. Once those thoughts are identified, Spruce helps you to look at your thoughts in a rational & sensible way.
